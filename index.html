HIPPO

Hippopotamuses (often called "hippos" for short) are large, semi-aquatic mammals native to sub-Saharan Africa. They are considered to be one of the most dangerous animals in the world due to their aggressive behavior and large size.

HIPPO AGAIN

Hippos are excellent swimmers and spend a lot of time in the water to stay cool and avoid the heat of the day. They are also great at holding their breath, and can stay underwater for up to 5 minutes at a time.
